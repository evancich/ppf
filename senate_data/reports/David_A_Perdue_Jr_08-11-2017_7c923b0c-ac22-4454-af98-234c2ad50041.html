I am no longer reporting the assets individually because the assets are wholly owned by the Georgia Tech Foundation and I do not have any input or control in how the assets are invested. However, I do receive taxable income from the assets. As such, I will continue to report the income associated with the charitable remainder trust on Part 3: Assets, as required by the Ethics in Government Act of 1978.
